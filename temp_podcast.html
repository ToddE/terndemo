Podcast

College builds your knowledge. Mentorship shows you how to use it.

Hosted by Tina Fox, award-winning entrepreneur and founder of TERN Mentoring, TERN Talks isn’t a pep talk about enhancing your resume. It’s about what really happens when you step off campus and into the real world — the messy, beautiful reality check no syllabus could possibly cover.

On the show, Tina talks with business leaders, educators, university alumni, and entrepreneurs who remember exactly what it felt like to have a freshly-printed diploma and no clue how to navigate the world outside of academia.

If you’re a college student, TERN Talks is here to help you build the mindset to navigate not just the start of your career, but any stage within it.

If you’re a university alum or professional, this is your invitation to give back — not for nostalgia, but for the version of yourself who needed a mentor.

Tina brings her full story: corporate leader, entrepreneur, mother, alumna, and lifelong learner.

TERN Talks is for anyone standing on the edge of what’s next — your first job, a career leap, a pause for family, or deciding to mentor someone else.

Tune in for real stories, surprising lessons, and perspectives that will help you see your path more clearly.

Join Tina in conversations that lift the next generation of professionals and leaders.

Join the community at ternmentoring.com and follow TERN Talks wherever you get your podcasts.
