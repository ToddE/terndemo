# Episode 1: The Future of Mentorship

In this episode, we sit down with leading educators and hiring managers to discuss the widening soft skills gap and how next-generation mentorship programs can bridge the divide.

## Key Takeaways
- **The Networking Deficit**: Why recent grads struggle to build professional relationships.
- **The Role of the '4th P'**: How professional mentors provide insights that professors, peers, and parents cannot.
