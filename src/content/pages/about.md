# Mission-Driven Modern Mentorship

The sphere of influence for most college students is typically the 3 P’s — Professors, Peers, and Parents. But what if there was a fourth P — another person or professional outside of the campus community — who wants to provide their time and talent to support a student through mentorship?

TERN Mentoring was founded on the belief that next-generation mentoring can bridge the gap between academic learning and professional success.
