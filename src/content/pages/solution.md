# Experience Next-Generation Mentoring

While students are graduating with a solid foundation of skills and knowledge related to their respective majors, they aren’t leaving campus with key soft skills most valued by employers. Specifically, many recent graduates lack even the most basic ability to successfully network and communicate in business settings.

For many employers, this is more than just a minor inconvenience; it’s a significant barrier to productivity and long-term success.

## Bridging the Gap

TERN Mentoring provides a structured framework to connect students with professional mentors who can guide them through the nuances of business communication, networking, and career development.
